## About Me

I am a data analyst focused on **healthcare operations, KPI reporting, dashboard development, and business decision support**.

My background combines hands-on experience in healthcare service operations with technical training in **SQL, Excel, Power BI, data cleaning, reporting, and business intelligence**. I am especially interested in projects that answer practical operational questions:

- Which hospitals, departments, or service lines need attention?
- What trends are driving performance changes?
- Where are the highest-cost or highest-risk areas?
- How can dashboards help leaders make clearer decisions?

My goal is to build analyst work that is not just visually clean, but useful, explainable, and connected to real business decisions.

## Featured Projects

### Oregon Hospital Readmission Analysis

**Healthcare analytics project using SQL and Power BI to identify readmission risk across Oregon hospitals.**

This project analyzes hospital readmission data to identify which facilities, conditions, counties, and CMS rating groups are associated with elevated readmission risk.

**Business Questions Answered**

- Which hospitals have the highest excess readmission ratios?
- Which conditions are most associated with elevated readmission risk?
- Do CMS overall ratings align with readmission outcomes?
- Which Oregon counties show stronger or weaker hospital performance?
- What operational actions could reduce readmission risk?

**Tools Used**

`SQL` `Power BI` `Excel` `Healthcare Analytics` `KPI Reporting`

**Skills Demonstrated**

- SQL joins and aggregations  
- Healthcare KPI analysis  
- Dashboard design  
- Readmission risk analysis  
- Executive recommendations
